About

I'm currently a Senior Research Scientist at NVIDIA Corp., where I actively conduct research on AI for Semiconductor Manufacturing. Prior that, I was a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research interests include (1) AI for VLSI EDA and Semiconductor Manufacturing (2) Optimization and GPU Acceleration and (3) Machine Learning Security.
