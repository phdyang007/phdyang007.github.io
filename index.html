About

I'm currently a Lead Software Engineer at Cadence Design Systems Inc, where I actively conduct research and development on Innovus product. Prior that, I was a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research interests include (1) Machine Learning in VLSI Design for Manufacturability (2) High Performance VLSI Physical Design with Parallel Computing and (3) Machine Learning Security.
